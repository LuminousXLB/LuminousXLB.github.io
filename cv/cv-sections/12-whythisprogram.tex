\lettersection{Why this programme?} % your reasons for applying to the programme

Tackling security problems is fun.
I chose to major in Information Security for my Bachelor's Degree only because it was far more difficult to get into a programme in Computer Science.
But after learning more about security, I actually found it interesting and meaningful to work on security and research security problems.
With the development of information technology, people are more and more dependent on computers and network.
Thus there must be someone dedicating into protecting the network application and infrastructure from attack and facilitating trusts between unknown people.

NUS is one of the most excellent universities in Asia and ranks top in Computer Security and Cryptography research.
During the NGNE Programme, I was impressed by its rich education resource and loved the rigorous academic atmosphere there.

Therefore, I am longing for furthering my study via the Master's Programme in Infocomm Security.
